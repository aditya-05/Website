KNOW YOUR DOCTOR

DR. PANKAJ BOOB

Dr. Pankaj Boob completed his M.B.B.S in the year 2001 from Dr. Vasantrao Pawar Medical College, Nashik. After that he completed his D.G.O and D.N.B in Gynac from K.E.M Hospital and Seth G S Medical College, Mumbai. Then he went on to work as a consultant Gynaecologist at Shrimad Rajchandra Hospital, in Dharampur in Gujarat. After working there for 4 years, he along with his wife Dr. Abha Boob, came to Kopargaon and founded DOCTOR BOOB HOSPITAL,SURGICAL AND MATERNITY HOME in the year 2009. He is the consulting Gynaecologist at Dr.Boob Hospital.

DR. ABHA BOOB

Dr. Abha Boob completed her M.B.B.S from M.G.M Medical College,Indore. Then she went on to do her Diploma in Anesthesia and Critical Care from N.S.C.B Medical College,Jhabalpur. After completing her Diploma, she went on to work as a Consultant in Shrimad Rajchandra Hospital, Dharampur,Gujarat. After working there for 4 years, she along with her husband Dr.Pankaj Boob came to Kopargaon and founded DOCTOR BOOB HOSPITAL,SURGICAL AND MATERNITY HOME in Kopargaon. She is the Administrator and Senior Anesthetist at Dr.Boob Hospital.
